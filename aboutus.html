About Us

Lila-Jo is a handmade collection of Pinup and Rockabilly accessories, specializing in floral hair clips and rockin handbags!


It all started in October 2006 and has grown to be my full-time handmade dream. I love creating pieces that I myself would wear, while paying attention to detail and durability. I'm the sole creator, maker and everything is packaged and shipped by lil' ol' me!


I've had my fair share of health issues over the years and everything about Lila-Jo has been my therapy. My dad once said, "If you can't find work, make work!" How lucky am I, that everyday I do what I love and it never feels like work.


I'm so thankful everyday for all the Lila-Jo customers and loyal fans!


Thank you so much for visiting my happy place!

XoXo,
Shana
