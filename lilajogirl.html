Lila-Jo Girl July 2021

Each month Lila-Jo features a woman who knows how to wear our stuff.


This month meet Kasumi Psycho.


She is a pinup model from Japan who likes to mix tattoos with the traditional pinup style. She describes herself as "the perfect mix of sexy and cool".


During the day you can catch her at her store, Psycho Apparel, where Lila-Jo pieces can be found, and at night DJing around Tokyo.
